Enjoy Delicious Food in Your Healthy Life.

Indian cuisine is characterized by its use of aromatic spices, herbs, and vegetables. Some of the most popular dishes include biryani, butter chicken, dosa, and samosas. The cuisine is also influenced by religious beliefs.

Fish Thali

A characteristic seafood thali here will comprise rice, fried fish, Mangalorean fish curry and fried prawns along with a full vegetarian fare.

Nonveg Thali

Non-vegetarian thali usually contains salad, rice, chappati and gravy or dry chicken/mutton dish.

Andhra Thali

Andhra Thali includes avakaya, gongura, pulusu, pappucharu, jonna kudu, bobbattu, kaja, and arisa. It uses spices. Rice is the staple food and is used in a wide variety of ways.
